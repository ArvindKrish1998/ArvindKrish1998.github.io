Huffman Coding (Python, C)

The binary strings can then be used in place of the ascii characters to ultimately create compressed files. Digital Multimeter (C)

I utilized a 16-bit ADC to create a multimeter than can measure DC offset, true-RMS, frequency, and peak-to-peak Voltages for various AC and DC inputs. I found that using a comparator circuit made finding the frequency much easier. Taking a continuous rolling average of the inputs worked best for the other values, as it increased the resposiveness of the multimeter when the input signal changes rapidly. I configured the output to read on a terminal with accompanying bar graphs.
